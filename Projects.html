CNC Router
[Image coming soon]
Inspired by my experience machining at the Baum Family Makerspace, I designed a desktop-sized CNC router capable of machining Aluminum. Using MasterCAM and Dxf files, I personally programmed and operated the USC makerspace's OMAX waterjet and HASS VF-2 mill to manufacture the parts. Limit switches were also hand soldered and implemented for added safety. Computer vision projects will be done in the near future to enable automated milling.
Analysis of Fin Separation on Convective Heat Transfer
Experimental setup for measuring temperature at the base, middle, and tips of fins. Fan with nozel (left) was used for forced convection.
To test the effects of fin spacing on heat transfer rate, I machined a custom modular heat sink and used a hot plate as a heat source. Forced convection trials were performed with a fan and nozzle and were compared with free/natural convection cases. Thermocouples were used to probe the temperatures at the base, middle, and tips of the fins. A custom LabView virtual instrument was used to visualize temperature over time and determine when steady state was reached.
MEMS Pressure Sensor
Fully assembled pressure sensor.
Applying concepts from the USC Introduction to Microelectromechanical Systems course (AME455), I collaborated with a team of four to create a macroscale MEMS pressure sensor. Strain gauges were adhered between two silicone rubber membranes, which were screwed down and sealed with glue. Strain gauge/ Wheatstone bridge readings were output on a voltage-time graph in a custom LabView virtual instrument.
Energy Generating Shoe Soles
Fully assembled wheelchair stabilizer with raspberry pi, Unitree A1 BLDC motor, and 24V battery.
Inspired by the winged shoes of Hermes, I set out with my USC Makers team to design light-up shoes powered by footsteps. After embedding piezoelectric crystals into 3D printed shoe soles, we used a bridge rectifier circuit to supply DC current to LEDs. We successfully powered 8 LEDs per shoe and presented our work at the USC Makers showcase.
